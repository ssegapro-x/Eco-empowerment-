Our Mission

Eco Empowerment is a student-led initiative focused on environmental awareness and practical sustainability projects at St. Kizito Katikamu Kisule Secondary School in Wobulenzi, Luwero District, Uganda. We aim to inspire change, one action at a time.

üå≥ Featured Project: Tree for Tomorrow

We planted over 200 native trees at St. Kizito Katikamu Kisule Secondary School and the community park in Wobulenzi, Luwero District, Uganda, to combat deforestation and educate others about local biodiversity.
